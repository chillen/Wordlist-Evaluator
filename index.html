Read the stimulus word on the left, then read the related list on the right. Each word should be related to the stimulus.

Beside each word is 5 buttons representing issues you may find with that word. Click each issue you feel is relevant, then press "Next".

The potential issues are:

  • Unrelated: The word has no apparent relation to the stimulus.
  • Synonomous: The word is synonymous with the stimulus.
  • Obvious: The word has a very obvious relation (eg. compound word) and fails to stimulate new ideas.
  • Dull: The word is boring, archaic, or meaningless, and fails to stimulate new ideas.
  • Other: You feel the word is out of place, but does not fit into any of these categories.
